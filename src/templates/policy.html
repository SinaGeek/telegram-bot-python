Privacy Policy

WOWDrive Bot - Last updated: December 2024

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how WOWDrive Bot collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

Authentication Data

We collect and store the following information to provide our service:

  • Google Drive OAuth tokens: Required to access your Google Drive for file operations
  • User ID: Your Telegram user ID to associate files with your account
  • File metadata: File names, sizes, and upload timestamps for progress tracking

What We DON'T Collect

  • File content or data
  • Personal information beyond what's necessary for the service
  • Usage patterns or analytics
  • Any data not directly related to file uploads

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Authenticating with Google Drive on your behalf
  • Uploading files from Telegram to your Google Drive
  • Providing upload progress updates
  • Managing your files (rename, delete) as requested
  • Displaying storage usage statistics

Data Storage and Security

Where Your Data Is Stored

  • OAuth tokens: Stored locally on our server in encrypted format
  • Files: Uploaded directly to your Google Drive, not stored on our servers
  • Metadata: Temporarily stored during upload process, then deleted

Security Measures

  • OAuth tokens are encrypted and stored securely
  • All communications use HTTPS encryption
  • Files are transferred directly to Google Drive without intermediate storage
  • Regular security audits and updates

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties. Your information is only used to provide the WOWDrive Bot service and is never sold, rented, or shared with other companies.

Your Rights and Control

Access and Control

  • You can revoke Google Drive access anytime from your Google Account settings
  • You can delete your authentication data by contacting us
  • You have full control over your files in Google Drive

Data Retention

We retain your OAuth tokens only as long as necessary to provide the service. You can revoke access at any time, which will immediately delete your authentication data.

Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with:

  • Google Drive API: For file operations (subject to Google's privacy policy)
  • Telegram Bot API: For bot functionality (subject to Telegram's privacy policy)

Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies only for:

  • Session management during OAuth authentication
  • Remembering your authentication state

We do not use tracking cookies or analytics.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@wowdrivebot.com
  • Telegram: @wowdrivebot_support
By using WOWDrive Bot, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
